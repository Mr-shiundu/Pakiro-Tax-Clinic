Our Services

Pakiro Tax Clinic may offer these services either individually or as part of a comprehensive accounting package tailored to meet the specific needs of each client.

Bookkeeping:

Recording financial transactions, including purchases, sales, receipts, and payments.

Financial Statements Preparation:

Preparing regular financial statements such as balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements.

Tax Preparation and Planning:

Assisting businesses in preparing and filing their taxes, as well as providing advice on tax planning to minimize tax liabilities.ie VAT returns, income tax returns,and withholding tax

Auditing Services:

Conducting audits to ensure financial statements are accurate and comply with relevant regulations.

Financial Analysis:

Analyzing financial data to provide insights into the financial health of the business and make recommendations for improvement.

Payroll Services:

Managing payroll, including calculating wages, withholding taxes, and issuing payments to employees.

Business Advisory Services:

Providing advice and guidance on financial and business-related matters to help businesses make informed decisions.

Accounting Software Implementation:

Assisting businesses in selecting and implementing accounting software to streamline their financial processes.

Compliance Services:

Ensuring businesses comply with relevant accounting standards, tax laws, and regulations.

Budgeting and Forecasting:

Assisting businesses in preparing budgets and forecasts to plan and manage their finances effectively.
